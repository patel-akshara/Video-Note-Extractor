 Hello everyone, today I would like to talk about the importance of time management for students. Time management is the process of planning and organizing how to spend time on different activities. Good time management helps students complete assignments on time, reduce stress and improve academic performance. One effective technique is creating the daily schedule. Students can divide their day into study time, breaks, exercise and personal activities. Prioritizing important tasks before less important work helps ensure that deadlines are met. Another useful habit is avoiding distractions. Mobile phones, social media and excessive screen time can reduce productivity. Certain specific study goals and taking short break can improve concentration and learning efficiency. Time management also contributes to a healthy lifestyle. Students who manage their time well can get enough sleep, participate in hobbies and spend quality time with family and friends. This balance supports both mental and physical well-being. In conclusion, time management is an essential skill that helps students achieve their goals, maintain a balanced life and become more productive and wishful. Developing this habit early lead to long-term success in education and future careers. Thank you.
